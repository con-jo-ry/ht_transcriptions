<div n="7" type="paṭala">
<p xml:id="HT1.7.1">
	atha cchommāpaṭalaṃ vyākhyāsyāmaḥ—
	<lg>
		<l>yena vijñāyate bhrātā bhaginī cāpi saṃśayaḥ || 1 ||</l>
	</lg>
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.2">
	<l>ekāṅguliṃ darśayed yas tu dvābhyāṃ susvāgato bhavet |</l>
	<l>kṣemamudrāṃ vijānīyād vāmāṅguṣṭhanipīḍanāt || 2 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.3">
	<l>anāmikāṃ tu yo dadyād dadyāt tasya kaniṣṭhikām |</l>
	<l>madhyamāṃ darśayed yas tu dadyāt tasya pradeṣikām || 3 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.4">
	<l>anāmikāṃ darśayed yas tu grīvāṃ tasya pradarśayet |</l>
	<l>paṭṭisaṃ darśayed yas tu triśūlaṃ tasya pradarśayet || 4 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.5">
	<l>stanaṃ darśayed yas tu sīmāntaṃ tasya pradarśayet |</l>
	<l>medinīṃ darśayed yas tu cakraṃ tasya pradarśayet || 5 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.6">
	<l>bhṛkuṭīṃ darśayed yas tu śikhāmokṣo 'bhidhīyate |</l>
	<l>lalāṭaṃ darśayed yas tu pṛṣṭhaṃ tasya pradarśayet || 6 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.7">
	<l>pādatalaṃ darśayed yas tu krīḍate nandakena tu |</l>
	<l>mudrāṃ pratimudreṇa bhedayet samayena tu || 7 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.8">
	<l>vadanti yoginyas tatra aho putra mahākṛpa |</l>
	<l>yadi mālāhastaṃ darśayanti militavyam iti kathayanti || 8 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.9">
	<l>mālābhipreṣitaṃ kṛtvā samaye tiṣṭha suvrata |</l>
	<l>bāhye tatra melāyāṃ divyagocaram āśritāyām |</l>
	<l>tatra melāyāṃ yad yad vadanti yoginyas tat sarvaṃ kartavyam || 9 ||</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.7.10">
	he bhagavan ke te melāpakasthānāḥ | bhagavān āha—
	<lg>
		<l>pīṭhaṃ copapīṭhaṃ ca kṣetropakṣetram eva ca |</l>
		<l>chandohaṃ copacchandohaṃ melāpakopamelāpakaṃ tathā || 10 ||</l>
	</lg>
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.11">
	<l>pīlavopapīlavaṃ caiva śmaśānopaśmaśānaṃ ca etā dvādaśa bhūmayaḥ ||</l>
	<l>daśabhūmīśvaro nātha ebhir anyair na kathyate || 11 ||</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.7.12">
	he bhagavan ke te pīṭhādayaḥ |
	<lb/>bhagavān āha—
	<lg>
		<l>pīṭhaṃ jālandharaṃ khyātam uḍḍyāyanaṃ tathaiva ca |</l>
		<l>pīṭhaṃ porṇagiriṃ caiva kāmarūpaṃ tathaiva ca || 12 ||</l>
	</lg>
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.13">
	<l>upapīṭhaṃ mālavaṃ proktaṃ sindhur nagaram eva ca |</l>
	<l>kṣetraṃ munmuni khyātaṃ kṣetraṃ kāruṇyapāṭakam |</l>
	<l>devikoṭaṃ tathā kṣetraṃ kṣetraṃ karmārapāṭakam || 13 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.14">
	<l>upakṣetraṃ kulatā proktam arbudaṃ ca tathaiva ca |</l>
	<l>godāvarī himādriś ca upakṣetraṃ hi saṃkṣipet || 14 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.15">
	<l>chandohaṃ harikelaṃ ca lavaṇasāgaramadhyajam |</l>
	<l>lampākaṃ kāñcikaṃ caiva saurāṣṭraṃ ca tathaiva ca || 15 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.16">
	<l>kaliṅgam upacchandohaṃ dvīpaṃ cāmīkarānvitam |</l>
	<l>koṅkaṇaṃ copacchandohaṃ samāsenābhidhīyate || 16 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.17">
	<l>pīlavaṃ grāmāntasthaṃ pīlavaṃ nagarasya ca |</l>
	<l>caritraṃ kośalaṃ caiva vindhyā kaumārapaurikā |</l>
	<l>upapīlavaṃ tatsanniveśaṃ vajragarbha mahākṛpa || 17 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.18">
	<l>śmaśānaṃ pretasaṃhātaṃ śmaśānaṃ codadhes taṭam |</l>
	<l>udyānaṃ vāpikātīram upaśmaśānaṃ nigadyate || 18 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.19">
	<l>divasaṃ caiva pravakṣyāmi yoginīnāṃ sumelakam |</l>
	<l>hevajre yoginītantre sarvasattvārthahetunā || 19 ||</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.7.20">
	he bhagavan ke te divasāḥ |
	<lb/>bhagavān āha—
	<lg>
		<l>pretapakṣe caturdaśyām aṣṭamyāṃ ca tathaiva ca || 20 ||</l>
	</lg>
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.21">
	<l>dhvajaṃ śastrahataṃ caiva saptāvartaṃ ca khādyate |</l>
	<l>kṛpām utpādya yatnena māraṇaṃ kriyate viduḥ || 21 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.22">
	<l>kṛpāhīnā na sidhyanti tasmāt karuṇām upārjayet |</l>
	<l>duṣṭāvatāraṇe sarvo vidhir mukhyāt pratiṣidhyate || 22 ||</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.7.23">
	tatraivaṃ mantavyam |

	<lg>
		<l>dinas tu bhagavān vajrī naktaṃ prajñā prakalpyate || 23 ||</l>
	</lg>
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.24">
	<l>nākāryaṃ vidyate kiñcin nābhakṣyaṃ vidyate sadā |</l>
	<l>nācintyaṃ vidyate hy atra nāvācyaṃ yac chubhāśubham || 24 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.25">
	<l>yathātmani tathā sattve tathātmani ahaṃ param |</l>
	<l>iti sañcintya yogātmā khānapānādim ārabhet || 25 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.26">
	<l>yāvanto hy aṅgavikṣepā vacasaḥ prasarāṇi ca |</l>
	<l>tāvanto mantramudrāḥ syuḥ śrīherukapade sthite || 26 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.27">
	<l>śrīkāram advayaṃ jñānaṃ heti hetvādiśūnyatā |</l>
	<l>rukārāpagataṃ vyūhaṃ ka iti na kvacit sthitam || 27 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.7.28">
	<l>yeṣāṃ yeṣāṃ tu jantūnāṃ piśitam aśnīyate budhaiḥ |</l>
	<l>te te sattvā vaśaṃ yānti vajrakapālayogataḥ || 28 ||</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.7.col">
	chommāpīṭhanirṇayapaṭalaḥ saptamaḥ ||   ||
</p>
</div>
