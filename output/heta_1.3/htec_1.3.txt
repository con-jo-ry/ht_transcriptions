<div n="3" type="paṭala">
<lg xml:id="HT1.3.1">
	<l>atha devatāpaṭalaṃ vyākhyāsyāmaḥ—</l>
	<l>prathamaṃ bhāvayen maitrīṃ dvitīye karuṇāṃ vibhāvayet |</l>
	<l>tṛtīye bhāvayen muditām upekṣāṃ sarvaśeṣataḥ || 1 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.2">
	<l>tasmāt punar api śūnyatābodhiṃ dvitīyaṃ bījasaṃgraham |</l>
	<l>tṛtīyaṃ bimbaniṣpattiś caturthaṃ nyāsam akṣaram || 2 ||</l>
</lg>
     
<lg xml:id="HT1.3.3">
	<l>repheṇa sūryaṃ purato vibhāvya</l>
	<l>tasmin ravau hūṁbhavaviśvavajram |</l>
	<l>tenaiva vajreṇa vibhāvayec ca</l>
	<l>prākārakaṃ pañjarabandhanaṃ ca || 3 ||</l>
</lg>
     
<lg xml:id="HT1.3.4">
	<l>prathamaṃ bhāvayen mṛtakaṃ dharmadhātvātmakaṃ viduḥ |</l>
	<l>yogī tasyopari sthitvā herukatvaṃ vibhāvayet || 4 ||</l>
</lg>
     
<lg xml:id="HT1.3.5">
	<l>svahṛdi bhāvayed rephaṃ tadbhavaṃ sūryamaṇḍalam |</l>
	<l>tatraiva hūṁkṛtiṃ caiva prajñopāyasvabhāvakam || 5 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.6">
	<l>kṛṣṇavarṇaṃ mahāghoraṃ hūṁkārād vajrasambhavam |</l>
	<l>vajravaraṭakamadhyasthaṃ hūṁtattvaṃ vibhāvayet punaḥ || 6 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.7">
	<l>hūṁkārapariṇataṃ dṛṣṭvā dveṣātmānaṃ vibhāvayet |</l>
	<l>vajrajanma mahākṛṣṇaṃ nīlapaṅkajasannibham |</l>
	<l>athavā nīlāruṇābhaṃ ca bhāvayec chradhayā khalu || 7 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.8">
	<l>vyomni bhaṭṭārakaṃ dṛṣṭvā vajrajanma mahākṛpam |</l>
	<l>pūjayed aṣṭadevībhiḥ sarvālaṅkāradhāribhiḥ || 8 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.9">
	<l>gaurī mṛgalāñchanaṃ dharti caurī mārtaṇḍabhājanam |</l>
	<l>vettālī vārihastā ca bhaiṣajyaṃ dharti ghasmarī || 9 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.10">
	<l>pukkasī vajrahastā ca śabarī rasadharī tathā |</l>
	<l>caṇḍālī ḍamarukaṃ vādayed etābhiḥ pūjyate prabhuḥ |</l>
	<l>ḍombyāliṅgitakandharaṃ mahārāgānurāgataḥ || 10 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.11">
	<l>candrāli kālimārtaṇḍa bījaṃ madhyagataṃ bhavet |</l>
	<l>sa eva sattvam ity āhuḥ paramānandasvabhāvakam || 11 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.12">
	<l>visphuranti svadehābhā gaganamaṇḍalacchādakāḥ |</l>
	<l>saṃhāryānayed dhṛdaye yogī dveṣātmako bhavat || 12 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.13">
	<l>nīlāruṇābhavarṇena raktabandhūkanetravān |</l>
	<l>piṅgordhvakeśavartmā ca pañcamudrair ālaṅkṛtaḥ || 13 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.14">
	<l>cakrī kuṇḍala kaṇṭhā ca haste rucaka mekhalam |</l>
	<l>pañcabuddhaviśuddhyā ca etā mudrāḥ prakīrtitāḥ || 14 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.15">
	<l>kruddhadṛṣṭi dviraṣṭavarṣākṛtiḥ |</l>
	<l>vāme vajrakapālaṃ ca khaṭvāṅgaṃ cāpi vāmataḥ |</l>
	<l>dakṣiṇe kṛṣṇavajraṃ ca hūṁkāroccāraṇātmakam || 15 ||</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.3.16">
	<l>śmaśāne krīḍate nātho 'ṣṭayoginībhiḥ parivṛtaḥ |</l>
	<l>śvasatīty anayā yuktyā śmaśānety abhidhīyate || 16 ||</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.3.17">
	caturbhujaś caturmāranirjitaviśuddhitaḥ pūrvoktarūpavarṇahūṁbhavaḥ prathamavāmabhuje narakapālaṃ devāsurānāṃ raktena pūritaṃ prathamadakṣiṇabhuje vajraṃ śeṣadvibhujābhyāṃ prajñāliṅgitam | vajravārāhī prajñā bhagavadrūpiṇī || 17 ||
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.3.18">
	ṣaḍbhujaṃ trimukhaṃ vāmaṃ raktaṃ dakṣiṇaṃ candrāruṇābhaṃ prathamaṃ nīlam | nagnaḥ pūrvoktavarṇarūpam | bhujānāṃ pāramitāviśuddhiḥ prathamavāmabhuje triśūlaṃ prathamadakṣiṇabhuje vajraṃ vāmadvitīyabhuje ghaṇṭā dakṣiṇadvitīyabhuje kartiḥ śeṣadvibhujābhyāṃ vajraśṛṅkhalāsamāpatyā | yadvad bhagavāns tadvat prajñā | savyāvasavye kartikapālaḥ | traidhātukātmakamṛtakākrāntaḥ || 18 ||
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.3.col">
	devatāpaṭalas tṛtīyaḥ ||    ||
</p>
