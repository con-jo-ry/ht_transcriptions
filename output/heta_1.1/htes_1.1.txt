<div n="1" type="paṭala">

<p xml:id="HT1.1.0">
	<pb n="2"/>oṃ namaḥ śrīhevajrāya ||
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.1">
	evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān sarvatathāgatakāyavākcittahṛdayavajrayoṣidbhageṣu vijahāra | (1)
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.2">
	tatra bhagavān āha | sarvatathāgatakāyavākcittahṛdayaṃ bhaṭṭārakaṃ guhyātiguhyataraṃ | aho vajragarbha sādhu sādhu mahākṛpa mahābodhisattva [bodhisattvasya] vajrasattvasya mahāsattvasya samayasattvasya [jñānasattvasya] hṛdayaṃ hevajrasaṃkhyaṃ śṛṇu | (2)
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.3">
	<l>vajragarbha uvāca</l>
	<l>vajrasattvo bhavet kasmāt mahāsattvo bhavet kathaṃ ||</l>
	<l>samayasattvo bhavet kena kathayatu bhagavān mayi || (3)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.4">
	<l>bhagavān āha |</l>
	<l>abhedyaṃ vajram ity uktam sattvaṃ tribhavasyaikatā ||</l>
	<l>anayā prajñayā yuktyā vajrasattva iti smṛtaḥ || (4)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.5">
	<l>mahājñānarasaiḥ pūrṇo mahāsattvo nigadyate ||</l>
	<l>nityasamayapravṛttatvāt samayasattvo 'bhidhīyate || (5)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.5a">
	<l>[bodhicaryāsamāsena bodhisattvo nigadyate ||</l>
	<l>prajñājñānasamāyogāj jñānasattvas tathāgataḥ ||] (5a)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.6">
	<l>vajragarbha uvāca |</l>
	<l>hevajran tu bhavet kena īdṛśaṃ nāmasaṃgrahaṃ ||</l>
	<l>hekāreṇa kim ākhyātaṃ vajreṇāpi kim [tathā] ucyate || (6)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.7">
	<l>bhagavān āha |</l>
	<l>hekāreṇa mahākaruṇā vajraṃ prajñā ca bhaṇyate ||</l>
	<l>prajñopāyātmakaṃ tantraṃ tan me nigaditaṃ śṛṇu || (7)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.8">
	<l>dṛṣṭyākrṣṭimahācchomaṃ sāmarthyaṃ bahuvidhaṃ viduḥ ||</l>
	<l>stambhanoccāṭanaṃ caiva sainyastambhābhicārukaṃ || (8)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.9">
	<l>yoginīnāṃ yathānyāyam utpattisthitikāraṇaṃ ||</l>
	<l>sāmarthyaṃ jñānavijñānaṃ devatānāṃ yathodayaṃ || (9)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.10">
	<l>prathamaṃ tāvad bhaved ekaṃ herukotpattikāraṇaṃ ||</l>
	<l>bhāvenaiva vimucyante vajragarbha mahākṛpa || (10)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.11">
	<l>badhyante bhāvabandhena mucyante tatparijñayā ||</l>
	<l>bhāvaṃ bhāvyaṃ bhavet prājña abhāvaṃ ca parijñayā ||</l>
	<l>tadvac chrīherukaṃ bhāvyam abhāvaṃ ca parijñayā || (11)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.12">
	<l>dehasthaṃ ca mahājñānaṃ sarvasaṃkalpavarjitaṃ ||</l>
	<l>vyāpakaḥ sarvavastūnāṃ dehastho 'pi na dehajaḥ || (12)</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.13">
	<l><pb n="4"/>vajragarbha āha | he bhagavan vajradehe katamā nāḍyaḥ |</l>
	<l>bhagavān āha | dvātriṃśan nāḍyaḥ | dvātriṃśad bodhicittāvahā mahāsukhasthāne sravante | tāsāṃ madhye tisro nāḍyaḥ pradhānāḥ | lalanā rasanā avadhūtī ceti | (13)</l>
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.14">
	<l>lalanā prajñāsvabhāvena rasanopāyasaṃsthitā ||</l>
	<l>avadhūtī madhyadeśe grāhyagrāhakavarjitā || (14)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.15">
	<l>akṣobhyāvahā lalanā rasanā raktavāhinī ||</l>
	<l>prajñācandrāvahākhyātāvadhūtī sā prakīrtitā || (15)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.16">
	<l>abhedyā sūkṣmarūpā ca divyā vāmā tu vāminī ||</l>
	<l>kūrmajā bhāvakī sekā doṣā viṣṭā ca mātarī || (16)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.17">
	<l>śavarī śītadā coṣmā lalanāvadhūtī rasanā ||</l>
	<l>pravaṇā kṛṣṇavarṇā ca surūpiṇī sāmānyā hetudāyikā || (17)</l>
</lg>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.18">
	<l>viyogā premaṇī siddhā pāvakī sumanās tathā ||</l>
	<l>traivṛttā kāminī gehā caṇḍikā māradārikā || (18)</l>
</lg>
     
<p xml:id="HT1.1.19">
	vajragarbha uvāca | etā dvātriṃśan nāḍyo bhagavan kīdṛśāḥ |
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.20">
	<l>bhagavān āha | (19)</l>
	<l>tribhavapariṇatāḥ sarvā grāhyagrāhakavarjitāḥ ||</l>
	<l>athavā sarvopāyena bhāvalakṣaṇakalpitāḥ || (20)</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.21">
	saṃvarabhedaś ca kathyate | āli-kāli-candra-sūrya-prajñopāya-dharma-saṃbhoga-nirmāṇa-mahāsukha-kāya-vāk-cittaṃ | evaṃ mayā || (21)
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.22">
	<l>ekāreṇa locanādevī vaṃkāreṇa māmakī smṛtā ||</l>
	<l>makāreṇa pāṇḍurā ca yākāreṇa tāraṇī smṛtā || (22)</l>
</lg>
     
<p xml:id="HT1.1.23">
	nirmāṇacakre padmaṃ catuḥṣaṣṭidalaṃ | dharmacakre aṣṭadalaṃ | saṃbhogacakre ṣoḍaśadalaṃ | mahāsukhacakre dvātriṃśaddalaṃ | cakrasaṃkhyākrameṇa vyavasthāpanaṃ | (23)
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.24">
	catvāraḥ kṣaṇāḥ | vicitravipākavimardavilakṣaṇāś ceti (24)
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.25">
	catvāry aṅgāni | sevāupasevāsādhanāmahāsādhanāś ceti (25)
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.26">
	caturāryasatyāni | duḥkhasamudayanirodhamārgāś ceti | (26)
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.27">
	catvāni tattvāni | ātmatattvaṃ mantratattvaṃ devatātattvaṃ jñānatattvaṃ ceti (25)
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.28">
	catvāra ānandāḥ | ānandaḥ paramānando viramānandaḥ sahajānandaś ceti | (28)
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.29">
	catvāro nikāyāḥ | sthāvarī sar<pb n="6"/>vāstivādaḥ saṃvidī mahāsaṅghī ceti | (29)
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.30">
	candra-sūrya āli-kāli ṣoḍaśasaṃkrāntiś catuḥṣaṣṭidaṇḍo dvātriṃśannāḍī catvāraḥ prahārā evaṃ sarve catvāraḥ | (30)
</p>

<lg xml:id="HT1.1.31">
	<l>caṇḍālī jvalitā nābhau || dahati pañcatathāgatān ||</l>
	<l>dahati ca locanādīḥ || dagdhe 'haṃ sravate śaśī || (31)</l>
</lg>

<p xml:id="HT1.1.col">
	sarvatathāgatakāyavākcittahevajrakulapaṭalaḥ prathamaḥ |
</p>
