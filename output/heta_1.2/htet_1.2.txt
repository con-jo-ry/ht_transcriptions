</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.2.21">

</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.2.22">
	<pb n="35"/>parasainyavināśāya khaṭikāsādhanaṃ vakṣye | khaṭikāṃ piṣṭvā pañcāmṛtena kuṭhāracchinnayā sārdhaṃ vaṭikāṃ kārayet | mantraḥ—oṃ vajrakartari hevajrāya hūṃ hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ | siddhyarthaṃ koṭiṃ japet | pūrvasevāṃ lakṣaṃ japet | tāṃ sādhya kamaṇḍalugrīvāṃ veṣṭayet | veṣṭayitvā bhañjayet | sarve śatravaḥ śirohīnā bhavanti | vajrakartarividhiḥ || 22 ||
</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.2.23">

</p>

</p>

<p xml:id="HT1.2.27">
